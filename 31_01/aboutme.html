About Us

Entheos is a 8 year old Web Design company offering professional and affordable web solutions of the highest quality, to clients all over the world. We are based in Bangalore, India.

Entheos means "God within" in Greek. Interestingly, the word “enthusiasm” has its origins from the same word. God is the source of our inspiration and enthusiasm and the root of the ethics and integrity we follow in this company.

Entheos offers the following :

  • Ready-made Website Templates - These templates include Flash website templates, CSS & HTML web templates, Flash intros, logos and corporate identities, swish animated templates, ecommerce website templates, wordpress blog themes and cms templates.
  • Custom Services - Our custom services include logo design, stationary design, website design, template customization & dynamic website development.
  • Low Cost Solutions - This includes Dreamweaver & Fireworks templates, Flash Photo Galleries, Fireworks logos & Coreldraw logos.
  • Free Resources - We offer many free web design resources including tutorials, templates, background images, galleries, Flash games etc.
